My work seeks to reveal our connectedness
with all things in the universe.

This beautiful universe is
one big living thing with the great consciousness.
Its whole being is affected by pain in one tiny part,
just like in the human body.

We humans are a tiny part of the universe.
But we all live as an important
part of the greater whole.

Any war on this planet
can cause great pain for each of us.

We are all connected.


All of my art is a tool,
a way to reveal and remember
this connection with the cosmos.

I want to convey this great sense of unity
and to spread the wish of love and peace
that comes from and belongs to all the lives within it.

And I want to talk about
the liberation of our beautiful senses
which sometimes we close down.

We are all united.
